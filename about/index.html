Our Story

We're on a Mission
to End the Snooze Button

RiseUp was born from a simple frustration: why is it so hard to just get out of bed? We built the solution we wished existed.

The Problem

The Snooze Button Is Winning

Every morning, millions of people lose the same battle. The alarm rings. They hit snooze. Ten minutes later, repeat. An hour passes. They're still in bed, groggy, guilty, and already behind on the day.

Traditional alarms treat the problem as one of volume or annoyance. Make it louder. Make it more irritating. But that misses the point entirely. The problem isn't hearing the alarm — it's having the willpower to get up when you're half-asleep and your brain is screaming for five more minutes.

The Solution

Replace Willpower with Action

RiseUp solves this differently. Instead of relying on willpower (which doesn't exist at 5 AM), we create a system where action is required. No action, no silence. It's that simple.

The alarm goes off. You tap "Start Mission." You must complete a verified task — do pushups, photograph your made bed, say an affirmation, find a random object in your house. Only then does the alarm stop.

By the time you've completed your mission, you're out of bed, you're moving, and you're awake. The hardest part of the morning is already behind you.

🧠
Why AI

Because Cheating
Defeats the Purpose

Other alarm apps let you tap a button, solve a math problem, or shake your phone — all things you can do in bed with your eyes half-closed. That's not waking up. That's just turning off an alarm with extra steps.

RiseUp uses AI computer vision, video analysis, and speech recognition to verify that you actually did what you were supposed to do. Take a photo of your bed but it's not made? Rejected. Say an affirmation while mumbling? Try again. The AI keeps you honest.
