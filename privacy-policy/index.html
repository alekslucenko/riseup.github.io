Privacy Policy

RiseUp: Alarm Clock Missions · Last Updated: March 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how RiseUp ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the RiseUp: Alarm Clock Missions mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account using Sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID identifier and, if you choose to share it, your name and email address. Apple may provide a private relay email address if you choose to hide your email.

Alarm and Mission Data

We collect and store the alarms you create, mission preferences, streak data, and usage history to provide core App functionality. This data is associated with your account so it can sync across your sessions.

Camera and Photo Data

Certain missions require you to take a photo or video (e.g., "Take a photo of the sky," "Make your bed"). These images and videos are analyzed in real-time by an AI service solely to verify mission completion. We do not store, save, or retain any photos or videos captured during missions. Media is processed transiently and discarded immediately after verification. No images or videos leave your device beyond what is necessary for this one-time analysis.

Subscription and Purchase Data

If you subscribe to RiseUp Premium, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information. We receive confirmation of your subscription status through RevenueCat, our subscription management provider, which receives an anonymous app user ID and purchase receipt data from Apple.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymized, aggregated usage data such as which features are used, mission completion rates, and general app performance metrics to improve the App experience.

Device Information

We may collect basic device information such as device model, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting and compatibility purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's core functionality
  • Manage your account and alarm settings
  • Verify mission completion in real-time
  • Process and manage your subscription status
  • Send alarm notifications at your scheduled times
  • Track your streaks and progress
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve app performance

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

We may share limited information with the following service providers who assist in operating the App:

  • Apple (Sign in with Apple & App Store): Authentication and payment processing
  • RevenueCat: Subscription status management using anonymous identifiers
  • Supabase: Secure cloud database hosting for account and alarm data
  • OpenAI: Real-time, transient analysis of mission photos/videos for completion verification only — no media is stored or retained by OpenAI or by us after analysis

We may also disclose your information if required by law, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of RiseUp, our users, or the public.

4. Data Retention

Account data: Retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

Photos and videos: Never stored. Processed transiently and immediately discarded after mission verification.

Subscription data: Managed by Apple and RevenueCat per their respective retention policies.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) and secure cloud infrastructure. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

RiseUp is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You may:

  • Access your data: View your alarm settings, mission history, and streak data within the App.
  • Delete your account: Request account deletion by contacting us at the email below. Upon deletion, all associated personal data will be removed from our systems.
  • Manage notifications: Control alarm and notification permissions through your device's Settings app.
  • Manage subscriptions: Manage or cancel your subscription through your Apple ID settings.

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR, including the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and the right to object to processing.

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to request deletion.

8. Third-Party Links and Services

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
