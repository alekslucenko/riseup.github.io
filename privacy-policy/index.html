Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 27, 2026 · Effective Date: March 27, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy ("Policy") applies to the RiseUp mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Services") provided by RiseUp ("we," "us," "our," or "RiseUp"). RiseUp is an alarm application that requires users to complete missions (such as doing pushups, taking a photo, making your bed, or saying a prayer) to turn off the alarm. This Policy explains what information we collect from users of our Services ("user," "you," or "your"), including information that may be used to personally identify you ("Personal Information"), how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy. If you do not agree with this Policy, please do not use our Services.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Policy at any time. We will notify you of material changes by: (a) posting the updated Policy with a new "Last Updated" date, (b) sending notice to the primary email address associated with your account, or (c) providing in-app notification. Your continued use of our Services after such notification constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy. We encourage you to review this Policy periodically.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide Directly

Account Information: When you create an account via Apple Sign In, we receive your name and email address (or a relay email if you choose to hide your email). You may also provide a profile photo or username.

Alarm Configurations: We store your alarm settings, including wake-up times, repeat schedules, selected missions, difficulty levels, alarm sounds, and morning notes.

Mission Completion Data: We record which missions you complete, your completion times, streak data, success/failure status, and session history for your insights dashboard.

Commitment Signature: During onboarding, you may provide a signature as part of a commitment feature. This is stored locally on your device and synced to your account.

2. Information Collected Automatically

Camera & Photos: Photo and video data captured during missions is processed by our AI verification system (powered by OpenAI) to confirm mission completion. Images are transmitted to OpenAI's API for analysis and are not stored permanently on our servers after verification is complete.

Microphone & Audio: Audio recordings captured during voice missions are processed using on-device speech recognition (iOS Speech framework) to verify you spoke the required words. Audio data is processed locally on your device.

Motion & Step Data: For movement missions, we access your device's motion sensors and step counter to verify physical activity. This data is processed on-device.

Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, app version, and anonymous identifiers for analytics and crash reporting.

Usage Data: We collect anonymized usage statistics such as feature usage frequency, session duration, and navigation patterns to improve our Services.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Supabase: Cloud database for user data synchronization across devices.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and payment processing.
  • OpenAI: AI vision API for photo and video mission verification.
  • Apple: Apple Sign In for authentication, App Store for subscriptions.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Services
  • Verify mission completion through AI analysis
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Track your streaks, statistics, and progress
  • Process subscription payments
  • Send important notifications about your alarms and streaks
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the user experience
  • Respond to customer support inquiries

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase's cloud infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. Local data on your device is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Mission photos and videos are processed for verification and not stored permanently. You can delete all your data at any time through the Settings screen in the app ("Reset All Data").

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain data processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@riseup.app.

Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.
