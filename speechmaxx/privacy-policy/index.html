Privacy Policy

SpeechMaxx: AI Speech Coach · Last Updated: April 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SpeechMaxx ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the SpeechMaxx: AI Speech Coach mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account using Sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID identifier and, if you choose to share it, your name and email address. Apple may provide a private relay email address if you choose to hide your email.

Audio and Voice Data

Speaking missions, AI phone-call scenarios, and voice-based features require the App to capture your voice through your device's microphone. Audio is transmitted to our speech and language processing services for real-time transcription and scoring. By default, raw audio is not retained after processing — only the generated transcript, scores, and coach notes are stored to your account. You may optionally save a session recording to your account from the post-session screen; those saved recordings remain private to your account and can be deleted at any time.

Session Data

We collect and store the sessions you complete — transcripts, scores across clarity, confidence, pacing, filler words, structure, and presence, coach notes, pathway progress, streak data, and session history — to provide core App functionality and enable progress tracking.

Subscription and Purchase Data

If you subscribe to SpeechMaxx Premium or purchase the Lifetime plan, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information. Subscription status is managed via RevenueCat, our subscription management provider, using an anonymous app user ID and purchase receipt data from Apple.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymized, aggregated usage data such as which features are used, mission completion rates, and general app performance metrics to improve the App experience.

Device Information

We may collect basic device information such as device model, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting and compatibility purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's core functionality
  • Transcribe and score your speaking sessions in real time
  • Generate AI coach notes, analysis, and per-dimension scores
  • Manage your account, pathways, streaks, and XP
  • Process and manage your subscription status
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve model accuracy

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

We share limited information with the following service providers who assist in operating the App:

  • Apple (Sign in with Apple & App Store): Authentication and payment processing
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and Lifetime purchase status management using anonymous identifiers
  • Supabase: Secure cloud database hosting for account, session, and progress data
  • OpenAI: Real-time transcription, language analysis, and coach-note generation. Audio is processed transiently and not retained by OpenAI or by us after analysis, unless you have explicitly saved the session recording to your account.
  • ElevenLabs: Realistic voice synthesis for AI phone-call scenarios. No audio you produce is used to train ElevenLabs models.

We may also disclose your information if required by law, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of SpeechMaxx, our users, or the public.

4. Data Retention

Account data: Retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

Raw audio: Not retained by default. Processed transiently and discarded immediately after transcription and scoring.

Saved recordings: Retained only if you explicitly save a session recording to your account; deletable at any time.

Transcripts, scores, coach notes: Retained as part of your session history until you delete them or your account.

Subscription data: Managed by Apple and RevenueCat per their respective retention policies.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) and secure cloud infrastructure. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

SpeechMaxx is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You may:

  • Access your data: View your session history, transcripts, scores, and progress within the App.
  • Delete your data: Delete individual sessions, saved recordings, or your entire account from within the App or by emailing us.
  • Manage permissions: Control microphone and notification permissions through your device's Settings app.
  • Manage subscriptions: Manage or cancel your subscription through your Apple ID settings.

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR, including the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and the right to object to processing.

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to request deletion.

8. Third-Party Links and Services

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
